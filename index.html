❤️ Reasons I Love You

Reasons!? They often say it in terms of love, people tend to make a bucket of lists of reasons why they love a person. But when I think about you, I realize love doesn’t come from a list. You are beautiful, smart, kind, loving. But those are the things I admire about you, they’re not the reason I loved you. I love you because you are you. If I had to find reasons, it would make my love sound conditional, as if it depends on certain qualities that could change over time. But my love for you isn’t a calculation or a checklist. It’s in the way I think about you when you’re not around, the way I want to share my days with you, the comfort I feel in your presence, and the future I imagine with you. It’s something deeper than reasons and stranger than explanations. So if you ask me why I love you, my answer is simple: I don’t love you for a reason. I love you because my heart chooses you, and if I had a thousand reasons, they would still never fully explain what I feel for you!
